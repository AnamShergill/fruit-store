Que.2. How do I track my order?

Ans. For orders shipped through Blue Dart, the delivery status can be checked online at http://www.bluedart.com by entering the tracking number for your shipment. The tracking number is provided in your shipment confirmation email.

The tracking number is provided in your shipment confirmation email. Orders placed after Friday at 10:00 AM will be processed for shipment on the following Monday.
